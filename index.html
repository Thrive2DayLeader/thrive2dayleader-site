About Rudy

A Framework Built
From the Inside

Rudy Bailey is the founder of THRIVE2DAY Leadership Lab and the creator of The 60-Minute Leader™ Framework — a leadership development system built not from textbooks, but from the wreckage of a life lived without margins.

His path to leadership was anything but linear. Rudy's journey through early career burnout, hospitalization, a return to school at forty while parenting three children and running a business, two cancer diagnoses, and a mini-stroke that arrived on an ordinary afternoon gave him an understanding of leadership capacity that cannot be learned in a classroom.

After rebuilding from nothing — including a season of borrowing a lawn mower and going door-to-door for work — Rudy entered healthcare leadership and spent nearly two decades leading complex, multi-site teams through staffing crises, organizational upheaval, a global pandemic, and every kind of high-stakes challenge real organizations produce. He co-created The Power of You™ training and drove team engagement from 69% to above 90%.

The Framework

The Six Reset Pillars™

The 60-Minute Leader™ Framework is a structured weekly reset practice built on six research-backed dimensions of sustainable leadership performance. Just 60 minutes — ten minutes per pillar — every week.

01

Clarity

Weekly recalibration of priorities, non-negotiables, and leadership focus.

02

Reflection

Honest assessment of wins, lessons, and patterns from the prior week.

03

Alignment & Purpose

Reconnecting daily work to deeper leadership mission and values.

04

Rest

Intentional attention to recovery, energy, and sustainable leadership pace.

05

Growth

A specific area for development and commitment to deliberate practice.

06

Connection

Evaluating and strengthening the key relationships leadership depends on.
